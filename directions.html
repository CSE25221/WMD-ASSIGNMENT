Locating Thari Lounge

How to Find Us from SSK International

We are situated exactly 1.5 kilometers east of the airport terminal exit roundabout, along Airport Road.

  • 🚗 By Shuttle: Free hourly pickup service directly outside the Arrivals Terminal.
  • 🚶 By Foot: A scenic 12-minute walk along the pedestrian walkway.
  • 🚕 By Taxi: Instruct your driver to head toward the Airport Corporate Park plot 401.
